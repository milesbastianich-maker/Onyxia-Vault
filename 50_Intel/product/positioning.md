# Positioning

## Category
**Preemptive Cyber Defense Platform.** Onyxia owns this term. Not "SIEM." Not "GRC." Not "XDR." It's the *layer above* those.

## The problem we solve (their words)

> "CISOs and security leaders lack a core solution to harness the power of data and AI so that they can manage, optimize, and demonstrate the business value of their cybersecurity programs and investments. They also face ever-evolving risk exposures, increasing personal liability, and growing data overload with existing security tools."

Translated for a sales conversation:
- Security teams spend 40+ hours/week manually pulling reports from dozens of disconnected tools
- Point-in-time data leaves them with lagging risk management + wasted budget
- There's no central source of truth for security stack efficiency + performance
- SEC cyber disclosure rules and personal CISO liability now force a solution

## How we solve it

Four pillars (latest marketing language):
1. **Data-Driven Continuous Threat Exposure Management** — automate tailor-made program assessments + benchmark against industry standards. Mobile app.
2. **Automated Business and Board Reporting** — communicate how security initiatives connect to org goals. One-click board reports, monthly/quarterly options.
3. **Security Stack and Asset Coverage Optimization** — find blind spots, identify gaps and overlaps, ensure ROI and alignment with frameworks.
4. **OnyxAI — AI-Powered Preemptive Risk Mitigation** — program trend predictions, smart suggestions, actionable AI-driven plans to reduce risk.

## Taglines to use

- "From Reactive to Proactive to Preemptive."
- "Turn Your Data into Power."
- "Many other tools create more noise. We allow security leaders to focus on strategic insights and then drill down to operational improvements."

## Who we sell to

- CISOs, security leaders, VP Security at Fortune 500 + leading enterprises
- Industries where Onyxia already wins: healthcare, insurance, financial services, critical infrastructure, manufacturing
- Buyer is CISO (technical champion) but economic buyer often includes General Counsel (post-breach), CFO (budget), CIO (infrastructure)

## How to talk about Onyxia (peer tone, no vendor-speak)

Good:
- "We sit above your existing security stack, normalize the data, and auto-generate board-ready reporting."
- "Our CEO Sivan Tehila is a former CISO. She built Onyxia specifically for this problem."
- "Clients report saving 160 analyst hours a month on manual reporting alone."

Avoid:
- "Unlock synergies" / "seamless ecosystem" / "robust solution"
- "Revolutionary AI" / "cutting-edge"
- "Game-changer" / "paradigm shift"

## Differentiation from adjacent categories

| Category | What they do | What we do differently |
|---|---|---|
| SIEM (Splunk, Sentinel) | Log aggregation + correlation for SOC | We sit above SIEM, measuring program performance not just threats |
| GRC (Archer, ServiceNow IRM) | Compliance workflow + policy tracking | We deliver the automated framework dashboard without the GRC workflow overhead |
| XDR / EDR (CrowdStrike, SentinelOne) | Endpoint detection + response | We measure and report their effectiveness, we don't replace them |
| Attack Surface (Randori, CyCognito) | External attack surface discovery | We correlate ASM data with internal program performance |
| Board reporting point tools | Dashboards only | We combine reporting + benchmarking + predictive AI + remediation recommendations |
