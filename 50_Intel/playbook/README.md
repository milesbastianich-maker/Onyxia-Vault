The compressed operating knowledge for Onyxia sales.

## Key rules pulled up to the top

1. Onyxia does not lose to competitors. Onyxia loses to silence. Engineer against silence.
2. Multi-thread every deal to ≥2 contacts within 30 days. 60% of losses are 1-contact deals.
3. Healthcare + critical infra + identity-SaaS are the winning verticals. 80% of wins + dollars.
4. Peer-intro play collapses cycles by 5-10x. Radiant Logic (36 days) vs WellStar (553 days).
5. Economic Buyer must be named before proposal. No exceptions.
6. Big enterprise losses often re-win later. Don't disqualify Tier 1 after initial silence.
7. Israeli entities: disqualify faster.
