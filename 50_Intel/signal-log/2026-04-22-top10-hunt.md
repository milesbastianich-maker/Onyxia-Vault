### 3. Hunt methodology lessons learned this session
- ZoomInfo `lookup` fuzzy-match returns almost nothing for industry/intent terms. Workaround: use industry strings from `enrich_companies` output directly, skip lookup for filter codes.
- Seed selection matters massively. 3 of 5 seeds (healthcare + insurance) produced gold. 2 seeds (small identity SaaS, small transit authority) produced noise. Future hunts should pre-validate seed companies against ICP rubric before running find_similar_companies.
- `search_scoops` with broad employee + date filters returns high volume of irrelevant exec moves. Layer job-title OR department filters tightly or the signal-to-noise is too low.
- `search_intent` `audienceStrengthMin` enum values are literals (A/B/C/D/E), not quoted strings. `employeeRangeMin` must be integer not string despite the schema indicating string.
- **Biggest territory finding:** of ~25 ICP-qualified healthcare/insurance lookalikes deduped against HubSpot, 22 are owned by Brad Baldelli (77002456). Brad owns the enterprise healthcare+fins territory. Miles's clean openings are almost exclusively in companies NOT YET in HubSpot at all.
