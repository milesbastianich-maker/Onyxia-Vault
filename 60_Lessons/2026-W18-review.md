## Lesson of the week

The pre-ghost pattern appeared twice this week at two different deal stages. At AKUVO, Dan McNamara opened a scheduled POC call with "refresh my memory" — then cited "other priorities" and pushed to late May. At Starboard Group, Robert Gunderson showed up to the re-engage meeting but left no debrief, no locked next step, and no colleague introduction despite Jose Betancourt being CC'd. Both contacts showed up when asked. Neither drove the conversation forward on their own. That is the tell. A real champion asks questions before the call ends, forwards invites to colleagues proactively, and names the next step without being prompted. A polite contact does none of that — they just show up. When this pattern appears, more follow-up to the same person is the wrong move. The correct move is an immediate second-thread attempt and an escalation angle to their manager. People Inc is showing the same early signal: $150K deal, 1 contact, no one returning calls, no named champion. Do not wait for a third data point.
